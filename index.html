About Us

A Legacy Woven in Every Thread

G.H.W. Knitwears Pvt. Ltd., an ISO 9000:2001 certified organization, has been a distinguished name in the hosiery business under our renowned brand, GRACEWAY. Established in 1962 by the late S. Kirpal Singh Chandoke, the company was built upon his extraordinary dedication and visionary approach, introducing numerous pioneering innovations to hosiery production.

Today, his son, S. Jagjeet Singh, seamlessly carries the baton forward. By integrating modern techniques with time-honored traditions, he has expanded our portfolio to include world-class winter wear solutions and home furnishing products that meet rigorous global standards.

Based in Ludhiana, India, we are a premier custom-made clothing manufacturer and fabrics export house. Graceway is widely recognized in the industry for an uncompromising, quality-driven attitude. We emphasize the strict maintenance of exceptional manufacturing standards for our caps, socks, stoles, shawls, and mufflers.
